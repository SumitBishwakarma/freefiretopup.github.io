How to Download

1

Copy YouTube URL

Find the video you want to download on YouTube. Copy the URL from your browser's address bar or use the "Share" button below the video.

2

Paste URL & Analyze

Return to TubeSaver and paste the URL into the input field at the top of this page. Click the "Analyze" button to process the video details.

3

Select Format & Quality

Choose your preferred format (MP4, WebM, MP3) and quality for the download. Higher quality videos will have larger file sizes.

Start Download

Click the "Start Download" button and your file will begin downloading automatically. Save it to your desired location and enjoy offline!

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service completely free?

Yes, TubeSaver is 100% free to use. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or payments required. We maintain the service through minimal advertisements.

Is it legal to download YouTube videos?

Downloading videos from YouTube for personal use is generally acceptable, but it may be against YouTube's Terms of Service. We recommend only downloading videos that you have permission to download or that are published under Creative Commons licenses. Never use downloaded content for commercial purposes without proper authorization.

What is the maximum video quality available?

TubeSaver supports video downloads up to 4K quality, depending on the original video quality. For audio, we offer up to 320kbps MP3 format. You'll always see all available quality options for each video when you analyze the URL.

Can I download private or age-restricted videos?

No, our service cannot download private or age-restricted videos from YouTube. We can only process videos that are publicly available without restrictions.

How fast are the downloads?

Download speeds depend on several factors including your internet connection, server load, and file size. Most downloads start within seconds and complete as quickly as your connection allows. For large files, such as 4K videos, it may take longer to process and download.

Can I download entire playlists?

Yes, TubeSaver supports playlist downloads. Simply paste the playlist URL and our system will analyze all available videos. You can then choose to download individual videos or the entire playlist as a batch.
